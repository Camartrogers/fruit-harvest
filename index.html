Instructions

Catch as many bananas as you can within 30 seconds. Click on the falling bananas to catch them before they reach the ground and get bruised.

Pineapples are pointy, you don't want to catch those, OUCH! Catching a pineapple will take away one of your 3 lives. If you lose them all then game over!
